Stochastic simulations
==================================
Stochastic simulations with compartment models and age structure. Has Gillespie and tau-leaping implemented.

`Link to example notebook for stochastic simulations of a generic user-defined compartmental model in PyRoss using Spp <https://github.com/rajeshrinet/pyross/blob/master/examples/stochastic/ex02-Spp.ipynb>`_
